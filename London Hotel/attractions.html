Local Attractions

London has it all: theater, shopping, and historic sites.

There are over 40 theaters in the West End, right on the doorstep of the West End Landon. When not attending a show, you can wander through miles and miles of the best shopping in the world, including Oxford, Regent, and Bond Streets. Feast your eyes on the endless choices of global brands and locally-sourced goods that abound in the upscale department stores and hip boutiques.

And don’t forget the sights, both historic and modern. Within easy reach of the hotel, you’ll find Buckingham Palace, Horse Guards, the Houses of Parliament, the London Eye, Saint Paul’s Cathedral>, the Tower of London, Tate Modern, and much, much more.
